World's Best Lasagna!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic,crushed
  • 1(28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2(6 ounces) can tomato paste
  • 2(6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italien seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan Cheese

Directions

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until it turns brown. Then stir in crushed tomatoes,tomato paste, tomato sauce and water. Add sugar, basil, fennel seeds, italien seasoning, 1 tablespoon of salt and pepper, and 2 tablespoon of parsley to the mixture. Let simmer for 1 1/2 hours.
  2. Cook lasagna in lightly salted water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. Combine ricotta cheese with egg, add remaining parsley and add 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degerees F.
  4. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce on the bottom of pan. Put 6 noodles over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Put third mozzeralla cheese slice on top. Put 1 1/2 cup of meat sauce over mozzeralla and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese. Repeat and add remaining mozzeralla. Add foil to prevent sticking.
  5. Bake in oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. 15 minutes to cool.
